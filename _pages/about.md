I am a postdoctoral research fellow in the [Astronomy and Astrophysics](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/physics/research/astro/) group at the University of Warwick. My research focuses on white dwarf demographics and atmospheres.

I completed my PhD in 2025 at Warwick, and was supervised by [Pier-Emmanuel Tremblay](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/physics/research/astro/people/tremblay/), and funded by the European Research Council. The major project of my PhD was using the Gaia satellite and ground-based optical spectroscopy to compile and analyse the largest volume-complete white dwarf sample to date - the 40 pc sample.
